The Yurts at Pacific Trails


What is a yurt?
Our luxury yurts are permanent structures four feet off the ground. Each yurt is
fully enclosed with canvas walls, a wooden floor, and roof dome that can be
opened.
How are the yurts furnished?
Each yurt is furnished with a queen-size bed with down quilt and gas-fired stove.
Your luxury camping experience includes electricity and a sink with hot and cold
running water. Shower and restroom facilities are located in the lodge.
What should i bring?
Most guests pack comfortable walking shoes and plan to dress for changing
weather with light layers of clothing. Its also helpful to bring a flashlight and a
sense of adventure.
